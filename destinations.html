Grand Egyptian Museum

Giza, Egypt

The Grand Egyptian Museum is the largest archaeological museum in the world dedicated to a single civilization, showcasing over 100,000 ancient Egyptian artifacts. It is located near the Pyramids of Giza and features the complete Tutankhamun collection displayed together for the first time. The museum presents Egypt’s history from prehistoric periods to the Roman era.
